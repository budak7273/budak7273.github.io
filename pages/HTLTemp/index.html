FAQ

Who Can Come?

Anyone can come! No experience is necessary, even to compete! We will have workshops for newcomers to learn as well as advanced programmers to hone their skills and help in the competition.

Is it an overnight event?

We'd like it to be, but we're not sure yet. Please check back later for updates!

What Does it Cost?

The event is completely free. Free food, snacks, and t-shirts will be provided for all participants.

What If I Don't Have a Laptop?

Please specify during sign-up that you will be needing a laptop! A limited number of laptops and computers will be available for use, but you must have a library card to check one out.

Can I Win Prizes?

Of course you can! There will be prizes for the top projects in each category! Stay tuned for details.

What Can I Make?

Anything you want, from games to websites to the next big social media platform. HackSTL is a time to experiment, get creative, and build something amazing.
